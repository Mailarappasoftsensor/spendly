Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Here is exactly how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

When you register for Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, and password. When you use the app, we also collect the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you add.

We do not collect payment information, and we do not track your activity outside of Spendly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and maintaining your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your personal information and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure and hosting. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only as necessary to support the service.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media trackers. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an update to this policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to responding promptly and transparently to any privacy-related concerns.

You can also review our Terms and Conditions for more information about your rights and responsibilities when using Spendly.
